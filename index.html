About Me!

Since then, I've gained a lot of experience programming and working with a great team of developers, testers, and designers! I've also taken a boot camp program from Thinkful to fill in some of the knowledge I'd have otherwise picked up in college.

Besides my job, I have lots of hobbies--I write, play guitar and bass, draw, have a black belt in karate and a brown belt in judo.
